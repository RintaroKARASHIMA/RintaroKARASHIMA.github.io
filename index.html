interest主な興味・関心

COMPLEX NETWORKS
複雑ネットワーク

Applying network science to understand the structure and dynamics of complex systems — from technological innovation ecosystems to corporate collaboration graphs. My research explores how connectivity patterns drive robustness, efficiency, and the emergence of macro-level behavior from micro-level interactions.

PATENT DATA ANALYSIS
特許データ解析

Mining large-scale patent databases to reveal the architecture of technological knowledge. By mapping citation networks and classification structures, I investigate how innovation complexity shapes competitive landscapes and drives industrial evolution — with a focus on quantifying technological diversity and specialization.

COMPUTER SCIENCE
コンピュータサイエンス

A broad passion for computational thinking — from algorithm design and data structures to machine learning and large language models. I am committed to translating mathematical rigor into practical, scalable solutions, and always tuned in to the latest advances in AI to stay at the forefront of technological innovation.

biography自己紹介

Cool Head, but Warm Heart

Rintaro Karashima is a Data Scientist at Hutzper Inc., a Japanese AI startup.
He completed his master's degree in 2025 at the Graduate School of Information Science, University of Hyogo, under the supervision of Professor Hiroyasu Inoue.
His research focused on quantifying and analyzing the complexity of corporation-technology networks, utilizing patent data, mathematical modeling, and network science to understand how innovation structures shape industrial evolution.
He earned his undergraduate degree in Social Information Science from the University of Hyogo in 2024, where he engaged in large-scale data analysis and marketing problem-solving using machine learning and statistical analysis.
