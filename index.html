COMPLEX NETWORK/複雑ネットワーク

Use methods from network science to analyze various relationships within society, such as social networks, technological grids, and biological signals. This field focuses on understanding how these networks evolve, their robustness, and connectivity patterns.

PATENT DATA Analysis / 特許データ

Explore technological innovation trends by mining patent databases. Analysts focus on patent classifications and citations to identify emerging technologies and market shifts, and to understand the strategic positioning of companies and inventors.

biography自己紹介

Cool Head, but Warm Heart

Rintaro Karashima is a first-year master’s student in the Graduate School of Information Science at the University of Hyogo, under the supervision of Professor Hiroyasu Inoue.
His research focuses on quantifying and analyzing the complexity of corporation-technology networks.
By utilizing tools such as patent data, mathematical modeling, and network science, he aims to understand the potential impact of these networks on various industries.
During his undergraduate studies from 2020 to 2024, where he earned a degree in Social Information Science from the University of Hyogo, he extensively engaged in large-scale data analysis and marketing problem-solving using machine learning and statistical analysis.
